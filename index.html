About Us

We are building a web3 tokenized and decentralized travel platform. Our expertise is in search related sites, and blockchain technology. 

We will be creating a DAO with rewards for users. A DAO is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization and is something fairly common in the web3 and blockchain space. A DAO can be described as a transparent governing body, where each member typically shares a common goal, which is the best interest of the platform.
